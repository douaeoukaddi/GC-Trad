ABOUT GC Trad

Exceeding Expectations. That defines our approach, and since 2019, GCTRAD has been committed to delivering innovative engineering solutions for every project.

GCTRAD specializes in building engineering, infrastructure, and urban development, offering end-to-end support for public and private clients—from feasibility studies and technical design to project management and on-site quality control. With a multidisciplinary and experienced team, we deliver reliable and efficient solutions, including structural and systems analysis, project planning and coordination, accurate cost estimation, and rigorous compliance inspections, ensuring quality, innovation, and performance across all projects, from small-scale buildings to large infrastructure developments.

history

Founded in 2019, GCTRAD aims to exceed expectations by providing reliable and innovative engineering solutions. The company has established itself as a trusted player in building, infrastructure, and development, serving both public and private clients. It has expanded its expertise to include technical studies, project scheduling, precise measurement, and on-site quality control. With a multidisciplinary and experienced team, GCTRAD manages a wide range of projects, from small renovations to large-scale infrastructure. Its mission remains unchanged: to deliver high-performing, innovative, and standards-compliant solutions while consistently exceeding client expectations.

Our Strengths

GCTRAD stands out for its ability to turn ideas into concrete, high-performing projects. Backed by a passionate, multidisciplinary team, the company excels in managing a wide range of projects, from meticulous renovations to ambitious infrastructure developments. Its holistic approach combines technical expertise, careful planning, and precise quality control, ensuring reliable and innovative results. Creativity and innovation are at the heart of every solution, enabling GCTRAD to tackle even the most complex challenges. Responsive, professional, and always attentive to its clients, the company positions itself as a strategic partner capable of exceeding expectations and delivering lasting value.
