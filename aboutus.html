ABOUT GC Trad

Exceeding Expectations. It defines how we approach our work and The Engineering Enterprise has been dedicated to exceeding our client’s expectations across thousands of diverse projects since 1974.

Our commitment to delivering on this promise has made us one of California’s leading electrical engineering and design firms. We’re well versed in both renovation and new construction ranging from small retail stores to large office campus facilities and much more. Our work spans a wide spectrum of projects including auditorium, civic, convention, data center, detention, educational, health care, hospitality, high-rise housing, industrial, laboratory, library, mixed-use, low rise and high-rise office buildings, parking garages, recreational, theatrical, retail and transportation facilities.
